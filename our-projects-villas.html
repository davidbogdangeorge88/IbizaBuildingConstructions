How to make a villa in 10 steps ?

1. Identification Of Needs And Expectations.

We have to make a summary with everything related to the client's wishes regarding the decor, investment, children's needs, expectations related to the decor, the number of rooms. The summary should also include both emotional and logical decisions related to this project.

2. Location Detection

You need to choose the right location, an environment in which to live as you wish. When choosing the location you must take into account the current situation of the region and its future. If you choose the location on a sloping land you must know that the costs will be higher and you should talk to us before purchasing the land.

3. Budget

In many cases, the construction cost exceeds the client's budget allocated for it. The construction of the villa costs more per unit area than the construction of a normal apartment. Therefore, you must correctly determine the budget you have and discuss these details carefully with us.

4. Architectural And Interior Design

If you want to build the house of your dreams, the relationship between you and the architect is a very important one. He must understand your wishes very well, and the relationship between you and him is reflected in the result obtained at the end.

The construction project must be discussed down to the smallest detail. Decisions related to the facade, interior decoration, or partitioning of the villa must all be established from the very beginning and be included in the allocated budget.

5. Engineering Solutions

The structural system of the building, the heating-cooling-ventilation systems, the sanitary installations, the electrical and lighting networks should be developed in accordance with the architectural design. Choosing sustainable systems, with low operating costs and minimal long-term problems, will contribute to the pleasure of villa life.

6. Execution of Legal Works [Permits]

Land registry directorate, land registry cadastre directorate, zoning and urbanism directorate, building inspection company, electricity-gas-water and sewerage-telephone, etc. many organizations will have to apply. The right way to get expert support and leave these issues to experts to carry out formal procedures.

7. Structural Construction

In the first phase of construction, the land is arranged. If it is not connected to any main road, it is necessary to arrange one. Then excavation and filling works are performed. Then the basic structural parts, the partition walls, the installation infrastructure, the walls and the garden sets, the pool curtains, the construction of the roof, etc. are built. The construction can be made of reinforced concrete as well as wood, steel, or stone.

8. Architectural Finishes

The structural construction phase is followed by the architectural finishing phase. This stage, as the name implies, includes decorative elements and detailing. Equipping wet areas such as bathrooms, toilets, kitchens, floor, wall, and ceiling coverings; fixed and movable furniture, roof, and facade claddings. Architectural Finishing can create huge cost differences compared to the materials to be selected. During the construction of the villa, the architectural style and character desired by the building owner come to life through the materials and applications to be selected. At this point, another important element is the selection of the groups that will be able to do their job correctly at the desired quality level. The experience of the architect who will manage the project is vital for the villa owners to reach their dream home.

9. Things To Consider In Villa Life

Life in a villa is different from apartment life. If you have small children, their safety is the most important issue. If you have a pool in the garden for example it is vital to make sure the pool is closed. In the case of a multi-story villa, separating panels can be used at the top of the stairs to prevent falling.

10. Villa As An Investment

Building a villa is a serious investment. When you are decided to do the construction, you must also take into consideration the fact that in the future you may want to sell the villa. A good idea is to make sure that what you are building now has value. Our advice is to choose a location that will increase in value as time goes on.
