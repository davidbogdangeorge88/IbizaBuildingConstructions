  • Excavators

We are a committed team with expertise and experience. We also provide a cost-effective and competitive service within the Eivissa region and surrounding area.

We can provide a range of services, from smaller domestic jobs such as driveways and pads to larger commercial jobs. We can also provide a vegetation clearing/clean-up service if required, just ask us! This includes but is not limited to:

  • Domestic and Commercial Landscaping

  • Foundations

  • Levelling and compaction as required for any work sites

  • Levelling of residential house sites and commercial sites

  • Earthworks for tennis courts and sports fields

  • Sub-contracting to Residential and Commercial builders

  • Demolition works – to include domestic and commercial buildings

  • Site Cleans

  • Tree Services

  • WE ALSO

    - We have all the necessary qualifications for our machinery and will provide all necessary approvals if required.

    - Follow all necessary Workplace, Health, and Safety guidelines ensuring that the site is safe both for our employers and yourself and the public.

    - Provide a responsible disposal service. All our waste is handled in an environmentally responsible manner and of course, we can leave anything behind if you think you are going to use it.

    • All type of excavation services that we can offer:

    Excavation is normally the first step when planning a new earthmoving job. This is actually the clearing of the existing area of things like rocks and vegetation and then changing the general shape of the landscape.

    Grading composes of the finer shaping of the land, ensuring it is ready for the next stage or contractors to start their project.

    Tree Clearing Have a large block that needs clearing from that pesky vegetation and al those trees? Have a smaller block that needs those annoying bushes cleared and a nice flat area in its place? This includes removing even the largest trees, stumps included!

    Demolition The good part about having a lot of heavy machinery is that we can use it to crush and break things. Sure we have to clean up afterward and take away the mess, but we enjoy that too!

    Construction Work Are you working on a building or construction site and need some quick plant work.

    Landscape Preparation Sometimes you just want your land to look better, and you need to start all over again. Or maybe you just bought a block and require some changes from the get-go. Dig some out here, add a little there.

    Pool Excavation One of our favorite jobs is providing a pool digging service, as we get to see how the excitement builds as families watch a basic hole in the ground get bigger and bigger in anticipation of what it will eventually become.

    Pricing Pricing can vary greatly depending on the type of job and the machinery used so we prefer you contact us and we can organize a rough quote over the phone if needed, or organize a site visit beforehand for a closer estimate.
