  • Welding Services

As a professional welding company we have equipped dedicated staff plus CNC plasma cutter, slip roll, benders, saws, grinders, drills, and a forge for fabrication.

We have the capacity to fabricate a wide range of items, whether large or small.

Materials that we use in welding:

  • Aluminum

  • Carbon Steels

  • Stainless Steel

  • Copper

  • Brass

  • Other Non Ferrous Alloys

There are varios type of welding that we can perform:

  • Welding iron structures

    Welding of steel, stainless steel, aluminum structures. Welding of all kinds of constructions of different sizes. We provide, for example, technological assembly of structures in the buildings of the automotive, engineering, and construction industries.

  • TIG welding

    TIG welding is one of the most popular welding processes. It produces high-quality welds but also requires a high level of skill. One of the greatest advantages of TIG welding is the amount of control it allows. A welder can control heat and amperage with precision using a foot or thumb remote control switch. We weld by TIG method for example airplanes in the aerospace industry, auto manufacturers, and sculpture welding.

  • MAG welding

    MAG (Metal Active Gas) welding is an arc welding process where an electric arc is created between a consumable wire electrode and the material to be joined. MAG welding processes are very versatile and we used them in a range of sectors, including the metalworking industry, shipbuilding, steel and container construction, and the automotive industry.
